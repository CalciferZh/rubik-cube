Built by Vica Yang and Calcifer Zhou. Thanks a lot to GitHub.

Solve: auto solve the cube use Kociemba's two-phase algorithm. Need to be initialized so may be a little bit slow at the first run =)

Build: color the cube freely. If the cube is legal, we can also solve it for you.

Scramble: rotate the cube randomly, and then begin your solving show!

Mobile: one finger to rotate cube, two fingers to change angle.

Desktop: left mouse to rotate cube, right mouse to change angle.
